About US

The Chunidevi Taekwondo Association was founded in 1969 in Lalitpur, Nepal by Siva Hari Budathoke, a former National Player in the Country Nepal. Budathoke's motivation was to establish a Taekwondo Dojo and to train students freely at the beginning. Chunidevi Taekwondo Association is one of the largest taekwondo organization in the Nepal. Also the one of the best training taekwondo association in the country. Claming a student of over 1500. Chunidevi Taekwondo Dozang is located at Bhaisepati, Lalitpur, Nepal.
